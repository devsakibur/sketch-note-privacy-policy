Privacy Policy for Sketch Note

Effective Date: July 24, 2025

At Sketch Note, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application available on the Google Play Store.

1. About Sketch Note

Sketch Note is a fast and easy-to-use digital whiteboard and sketching app built for productivity, creativity, and on-the-go note-taking. The app is designed for offline use and does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from its users.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, track, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identity (name, email, phone number)
  • Device location or IP address
  • Payment or billing data
  • Media, contacts, calendar, or files
  • App usage or behavioral data

3. Permissions

Sketch Note does not request any device permissions such as camera, storage access, microphone, or location. This helps keep the app secure and free of unnecessary risks.

4. No Internet Connectivity

The app works completely offline. It does not connect to the internet or to any cloud-based service. Your sketches and notes are stored locally on your device only (if saved).

5. No Ads or Third-Party SDKs

Sketch Note does not include any advertising libraries, analytics tools, or third-party SDKs that could collect user information or activity data.
